About Me

My name is Courtney Davis.

I grew up in Ahoskie, a small town in eastern North Carolina. From there I attended the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and earned a degree in Journalism.

I now live in Charlotte, NC, with my husband, Steven, my adorable dog, Lexi, and our two cats: Mittens and Tybalt. If you'd like to see some photos of them, check out the gallery.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing video games, listening to fantasy audio books and trivia podcasts, sewing, watching comic book-themed movies and TV shows, and reading personal finance blogs.
